Riyadh’s most walkable city neighborhood.

Diriyah will celebrate traditional Najdi inspired residences to inspire a past meet present feeling. Nestled within Riyadh’s most walkable city, the vibrant neighborhoods will cater to the resident’s everyday needs.

Thoughtful community living, set within highly walkable traditional Najdi streetscapes and in close proximity to everyday needs, Diriyah will offer luxury lifestyle retail and dining experiences, entertainment events and activated outdoor spaces. A holistic way of living to promote wellness and active lifestyle. The luxury residences, consisting of traditionally styled apartments, courtyard villas and townhouses, will be developed with the latest international and local sustainable standards designed for 21st century living.
