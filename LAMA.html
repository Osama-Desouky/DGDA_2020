Land and Municipal Affairs

The Land and Municipal Affairs department (LAMA) is mandated to develop and enforce regulations and guidelines for the development, designs, construction, licensing and facilities operations activities to ensure maintaining the highest quality of life standards implemented and maintained throughout the supervisory area outlined below.

As a part of the mandate, LAMA is entrusted to offer the highest quality of services to the community residents within the DGDA supervisory area. Three divisions lead the services provided to the community and are the Urban Planning division, the Land Management Division, and the Development Permitting Division.

Development
Permitting

Regulate and issue permits as well as approve the standards of design, building, infrastructure and the quality of implementation.

Urban
Planning

Approve and improve the planning structure and how land is used and infrastructure is incorporated to ensure best life quality and sustainability.

Land
Management

Their ultimate objective is to facilitate the supervision or transfer of ownership or land with the utmost comfort and ease for the citizen. Evaluate land, arrange acquisition, manage land records and transfer ownership.
